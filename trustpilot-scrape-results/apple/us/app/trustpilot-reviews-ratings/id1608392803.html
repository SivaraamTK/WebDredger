    Description

    Shopping online? Get peace of mind with Trustpilot, the world’s largest independent review platform for online shopping and services. With more than 190 million reviews — and a new one posted every second — you can buy with confidence no matter what you’re looking for.


    Use Trustpilot to:


    Shop smarter

    - Browse by best ranking companies for any product or service — whether you’re buying furniture or selling a car

    - Sort results by most relevant, highest number of reviews, or recency, and filter by location, number of reviews, time period, or company status-

    - Search reviews for keywords to find what matters most

    - Filter reviews by language, star rating, or popular mentions


    Get genuine guidance

    - Read authentic, unfiltered reviews from millions of people

    - Look up specific companies to see how they rank and interact with customers


    Share your experience

    - Write reviews for companies whose products or services you've bought or used

    - Inform fellow customers and help businesses improve with your direct feedback


    Discover new brands

    - Browse by best companies in a category, sub-category, or location

    - Email, call, or get directions to a business directly from the app


    Find companies you can trust

    - Verified companies, reviewers, and experiences to help you cut through the noise

    - Warnings and alerts to protect you from scams or misuse


    Need help? Visit https://support.trustpilot.com
